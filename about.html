Our Mission

We are on a mission to build the world's most powerful productivity tools that respect developer privacy by default.

The Story

It started in 2024 when our founding engineering team audited the network traffic of popular time-tracking applications. We were horrified to discover that every window focus change, every idle minute, and sometimes even active window titles were being continuously streamed to third-party cloud servers.

These tools were built for managers to spy on their employees, not for engineers trying to genuinely understand and optimize their deep work.

So, we threw out our subscriptions and built Coderoller. We chose Rust for absolute maximum performance and SQLite to guarantee that your data stays on your machine. No telemetry. No spyware. Just blazing fast, beautiful insights into how you spend your time.

Core Values

Privacy First

Your data is your own. We do not track you, we do not sell your data, and our core product works 100% offline.

Absolute Speed

A background tool shouldn't drain your laptop battery. We ruthlessly optimize for CPU and memory efficiency.

Open Ecosystem

We provide simple APIs and local database access so you can build your own dashboards and integrations.
