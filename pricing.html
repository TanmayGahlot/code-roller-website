Frequently Asked Questions

Can I use Coderoller completely offline?

Yes! The CLI Basic tier requires zero internet connection. All your data is stored locally in your SQLite database. Only the Cloud Pro tier connects to our sync servers.

Is my data secure in the Cloud Pro tier?

Absolutely. We use End-to-End (E2E) encryption. Your time data is encrypted with a local key before it ever leaves your machine, meaning we physically cannot read your tracking data.
